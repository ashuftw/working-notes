  
## Definition 
It is a square matrix where all elements except the diagonal, sub-diagonal and super-diagonal elements are zero i.e. $a_{i j}=0$ for $|i-j|>1$

$$
A=\left(\begin{array}{ccccc}
a_1 & c_1 & & & \\
b_2 & a_2 & c_2 & & \\
& \ddots & \ddots & \ddots & \\
& & \ddots & \ddots & c_{n-1} \\
& & & b_n & a_n
\end{array}\right)
$$
