  

## Ingredients 
- Flour 15 gm 
- Corn Starch 10 gm  
- Banana 1/2
- Chocolate Protein Powder / Vegan Protein 15 gm 
- Unflavored Protein Powder 20 gm
- Cocoa Powder 4 gm 
- Chocolate chunks 
- Baking powder 1/2 tspn 
- Baking Soda a pinch 
- Cider Vinegar / Lemon juice 1 tsp

- Mix and Microwave for a minute and half.
