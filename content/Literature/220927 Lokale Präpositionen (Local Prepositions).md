Prepositions answer to the questions **Wo** or **Wohin**. Hence they are also called Preposition of Place.

| Bispiele|  |
|---|---|
| auf | on |
| an | at, by |
| neben | beside, next to |
| vor | front |
| hinter | behind |
| zwischwen | between |
| über | over |
