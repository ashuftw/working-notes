## Use case
Describes the transport of a quantity by flow. 

$$
\boxed{
\frac {\partial u} {\partial t} + c \frac {\partial u} {\partial x }=0
}
$$

where, 
- $c\rightarrow$is the convection velocity.
- $u\rightarrow$is the transported quantity.
