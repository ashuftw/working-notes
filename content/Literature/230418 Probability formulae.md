1. **Unconditional or Marginal Probability**
	- **Overlapping Events** 
$$
P(A \cap B)=P(A \mid B) P(B)=P(B \mid A) P(A)
$$

- **Disjoint events**
$$
P(A \cap B)=0
$$

	- **Independent events** 
$$
P(A \cap B)=P(A) P(B)
$$


2. **Conditional probability**
	- **Overlapping events** 
$$
P(A \mid B)=\frac{P(A \cap B)}{P(B)}
$$

	- **Disjoint Events** 
$$
P(A \mid B)=0
$$

	- **Independent events** 
$$
P(A \mid B)=P(A)
$$

$$
P(A \mid A)=1
$$

3. **Bayes Rule**

$$
P(A \mid B)=\frac{P(B \mid A) P(A)}{P(B)}
$$
