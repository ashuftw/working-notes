# The Deborah and Weissenberg numbers
**Deborah Number**

$$
D=\dfrac{\text{time of relaxation}}{\text{time of observation}}
$$

High Deborah numbers, material behaves like a solid.
Low Deborah numbers, material behaves like a fluid.
