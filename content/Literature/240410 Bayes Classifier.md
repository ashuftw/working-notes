### Using [[240401 Bayes Theorem|Bayes Theorem]]

$$
\boxed{P(s=i \mid \mathbf{x})=\frac{p(\mathbf{x} \mid s=i) P(s=i)}{p(\mathbf{x})} }\quad i \in \mathcal{S}=\{1, \ldots, N\}
$$

- $P(s=i)$: **Prior**
- $p(\mathbf{x} \mid s=i)$: **Likelihood**
- $P(s=i \mid \mathbf{x})$: **Posterior** 
- $p(\mathbf{x})$: **Evidence**
