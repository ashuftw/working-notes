# 220827 Distinction between Present and Continuous Tense
In German, there is no distinction between the two. 

Example:

$$
\text{Er trinkt Bier}= 
\begin{cases}
    \text{He drinks beer.}\\
    \text{He is drinking beer.}
\end{cases}
$$
